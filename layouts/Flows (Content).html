Contract Leader (delegated Records Lead Responsabilities) > Contract Start
  • 1 CFM enters Contract Leader in SAP VA43 field which notifies ARIM team
  • 2 ARMS sends an email to the Contract Leader describing their responsibilities under Policy 0123AVA
  • 3 Complete RM Training for Contract Leader & Review Policy 0123AVA & Procedure 0123AVA.100
  • 4 Assign Records Lead in ARMS
  • 5 Send personal message to RL to announce new role & upcoming deadlines
  • 6 Ensure RL takes training
  • 7 Ensure contract submission within 30 days of contract start date in ARMS
  • 8 Work with the RL & review Records Retention Schedule to identify all required Customer Records pertinent to engagement & develop procedures for collecting and submitting Customer Records
